About The Event

The word 'Chrysalis' refers to a transitional state of a caterpillar before it turns into a beautiful butterfly. When we look at Chrysalis 2023, it embarks the beginning for some of us and the transition to a mature beautiful person for others. Being a part of our wonderful M.C.A department for these enchanting years, now is the time for us students to give a heartfelt gratitude to our department.

This time, 'Chrysalis 2023' is not just an event, but an ultimate platform for you to show your talent and prove your mettle. We will host a 4-day event starting from 5th January to 8th January. All events are being managed by students of M.C.A department.

Where

Shri Ramdeobaba College Of Engineering And Management, Nagpur

When

Thursday to Sunday
05-08 January
